## Description (max 4000 characters)

Vocabulary learning that's actually fun — and actually works.

VocabSpark uses a proven flashcard method (spaced repetition) so words stick for the long run. Cards you haven't nailed yet come back more often. Cards you know cold come back less.

FEATURES

• 16 languages to learn — Spanish, French, German, Italian, and many more
• Natural pronunciation — hear every word in a clear, human-sounding voice
• Automatic example sentences — see each word used in a simple, real-world sentence
• Translation helper — type a word and get the translation instantly
• Vocab Test mode — pick a set of words and drill them until they stick
• CSV import — bring in a whole word list at once
• Time filters — review only today's, this week's, or this month's words
• Streak tracker — keep an eye on your learning streak
• Daily reminder — so you never miss a day

HOW IT WORKS

1. Pick a language and add your words
2. Start a session — the app shows you a word
3. Reveal the answer and rate yourself: Again, Close, or Got it!
4. "Again" comes right back, "Close" comes sooner than "Got it!", which is done for now

MADE FOR STUDENTS

VocabSpark is deliberately simple. No complicated settings, no distractions — just you and your words. Perfect for prepping for the next vocab quiz.

NOTE

Pronunciation and example sentences require an OpenAI API key (enter it in Settings). All your data stays on your device — no accounts, no tracking, no ads.
